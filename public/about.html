CALCULATE YOUR BMI What is BMI

BMI is a reliable indicator of body fatness for most people. It is used to screen for weight categories that may lead to health problems. This calculator provides BMI and the corresponding weight category. Use this calculator for adults, 20 years old and older.







Body Composition:

Your BMI Check!

BMI is used to assess risk for diseases, especially heart disease and diabetes.

  • Underweight: Less than 18.5
  • Recommended: 18.6 to 24.9
  • Overweight: 25.0 to 29.9
  • Obese: 30 or greater

Body Fat percentage Check!

Body fat percentage distinguishes fat from muscle and calculates the percentage of body fat in the body.

Women

  • Essential Fat: 0-12 percent
  • Athletes: 14-20 percent
  • Fitness: 21-24 percent
  • Acceptable: 25-31 percent
  • Obese: 32 percent+

Men

  • Essential Fat: 2-4 percent
  • Athletes: 6-13 percent
  • Fitness: 14-17 percent
  • Acceptable: 25-31 percent
  • Obese: 32 percent+

Idea Weight Check!

Ideal body weight (IBW) is defined as weight for height at the lowest risk of mortality.

Calories Burn Check!

Your body burns calories through physical activity—taking a walk or climbing a flight of stairs.
